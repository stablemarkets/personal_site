What I’m About…

I’m a biostatistics PhD candidate at the University of Pennsylvania. I am also an Associate Fellow at the LDI and a member of of the Center for Causal Inference. For the most part, my research centers around developing Bayesian nonparametric methods for causal inference problems. Before Penn, I was a senior analyst at Analysis Group.

I blog about statistics, bayesian methods, computation/MCMC, visualization, and other things that happen to interest me.

My blog posts are syndicated on R-bloggers.
